Chickpea Summer Salad

Image by Anna Pelzer on Unsplash

Need a quick, filling, fresh Summer salad? Then this is it. This chickpea salad is easy to throw together and it's known to turn many cellery fanatics into cellery fans. Myself included.

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas
  • Cellery
  • Red Onions
  • Fresh Dill
  • Lemon
  • Dijon Mustard
  • Acacia Honey
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt & Pepper

Step by step

  1. Rinse a can of chickpeas and throw them in a bowl.
  2. Chop thin slices of a small red onion and throw into the mix.
  3. Chop a couple of cellery sticks into very small pieces and throw in the bowl.
  4. Chop the fresh dill, throw in the big mix, and set the big bowl aside for now.
  5. Then squeeze a half to a whole lemon into a small bowl.
  6. Add some olive oil to the small bowl.
  7. Add about a teaspoon or more of dijon mustard into the small bowl.
  8. Then add a squeeze of acacia honey to the mix.
  9. Lastly, add a dash of salt and pepper and mix everything together in the small bowl.
  10. Now add the dressing mix from the small bowl into the big bowl and mix it all together.
  11. Enjoy!
